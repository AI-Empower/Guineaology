Daily routine

Choose a moment in the care day.

Tap through the rhythm to see what to check, refresh, and record. It works as a quick primer for new helpers too.

Now viewing

Start the day with a full-body once-over

Catch subtle changes while your guinea pigs are most alert. Five unrushed minutes let you note weight trends, breathing, and appetite before the day gets busy.

Ambient targets

18-24 deg C | 40-70% RH

Guinea pigs are sensitive to drafts; open windows only if temperatures stay within range.

Hay refresh

Timothy + orchard mix

Top up at least two hay stations to keep fibre intake constant and prevent guarding.

Vitals glance

Weight & droppings

Record weights weekly - more than +/-30 g in a week warrants a vet call.

  • ↣Swap in fresh filtered water and confirm bottles release a steady drip.
  • ↣Offer 1/8 cup of fortified pellets per pig after you verify weights.
  • ↣Inspect eyes, nose, teeth, and coat for discharge or puffing.

Affirmation

Consistent morning touchpoints catch small changes before they become emergencies.
